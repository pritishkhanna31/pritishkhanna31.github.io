AI in Consulting — Client Engagement Research

PwC Ireland · Technology Consulting
2026 · UCD Smurfit MSc Management Consultancy
Real Client Brief

PwC Ireland came to our team with a real question: how should they be using AI to improve how they work with clients? I contributed to the research and analysis for a full consulting engagement delivered to PwC's Technology Consulting leadership team.

The work involved designing and running a survey of 30 Irish consulting professionals, conducting expert interviews including with a 30-year consulting veteran, and benchmarking what Accenture, McKinsey, EY and Deloitte are actually doing with AI right now.

The finding that shaped everything: not one respondent cited technology as their primary barrier. Every blocker named was a management decision. The gap is not technical. It is organisational. Delivered strategic recommendations to PwC Ireland leadership and presented to a room of directors and senior leaders.

How I Work

Respect, recognition and reward flow out of performance.

I take ownership, fully.

I do not wait to be told what to do. I look at a problem, figure out the best path and move ahead. Whether it is a team of one or leading others, the result is my responsibility and I treat it that way.

I do not just do the work. I improve it.

When I take on a task I do not just complete it the way it has always been done. I look for the smarter approach, the more effective route, the version that actually works better. That habit is not something I switch on. It is always running.

I do not compromise on quality. Ever.

I hold my work to a standard that sometimes feels unreasonable even to me. I have handed things in knowing they were better than what anyone else would have produced and still wondered if I could have done more. That is not self-doubt. That is a standard worth keeping.

I am better with freedom than with a manual.

Give me a problem, a deadline, space to think and watch me deliver something that surprises you. I thrive in environments that thrust people to figure it out rather than follow a script. That is where I do my best work.
